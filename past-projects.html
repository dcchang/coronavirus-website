I created a challenge for students at the University of Michigan to build a better campus in the face of COVID-19. Over 600 students from across the university came together to create novel solutions to problems that the university will encounter in the fall amid social distancing restrictions.
I co-founded MedLaunch, an engineering design team that builds assistive technologies for disabled people in the local Ann Arbor community. Each year, students compete in the Biodesign Challenge to create novel technologies for different community partners, with the potential to turn their projects into viable startups.
