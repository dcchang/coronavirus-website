Past Projects
I created a challenge for students at the University of Michigan to build a better campus in the face of COVID-19. Over 600 students from across the university competed in the challenge to create innovative solutions to problems that the university will face in the Fall semester because of COVID-19. These problems include devising ways to safely transport students between north and central campus, potentially housing thousands of students in dorms, and building community and helping students maintain good mental health in the absence of traditional large social gatherings.
I co-founded MedLaunch, an engineering design team that builds assistive technologies for disabled people in the local Ann Arbor community. Each year, students compete in the Biodesign Challenge to create novel technologies for different community partners, with the potential to turn their projects into viable startups.
Perch is a startup that connects students to academic and industrial research jobs. I was responsible for business development, which included customer discovery, marketing and growth.
